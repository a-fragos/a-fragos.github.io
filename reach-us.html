How to reach us

By local transport

To reach us you can use the public transportation (buses). Some basic and probably useful routes are the following:

From Paroikia Port:

  • 🚍 You board the "Paroikia - Naoussa" bus and get off at the "Naoussa, terma" stop (ie the Naoussa terminal).
  • 🚍 From Naoussa you board the "Naoussa - Santa Maria" bus and get off at the nearest stop to our apartments.

From the Airport

  • 🚍 You board the "Airport - Paroikia" bus and get off at the "Parοikia, terma" stop (i.e. the Parοikia intercity bus terminal).
  • 🚍 You board the "Paroikia - Naoussa" bus and get off at the "Naoussa, terma" stop (ie the Naoussa terminal).
  • 🚍 From Naoussa you board the "Naoussa - Santa Maria" bus and get off at the nearest stop to our apartments.

More information about the buses of Paros can be found here: https://ktelparou.gr/bus-schedules.html

By renting a vehicle

Each village offers automobile and motorbike rentals. Peruse the local guides and make your selection. There are motorized vehicles for all tastes and to fulfill every requirement! Agencies can be found at the entrance to villages and at the port.
